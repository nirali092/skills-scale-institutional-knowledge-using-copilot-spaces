# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Testing Lead

### Role Summary
The QA / Testing Lead ensures quality throughout the delivery lifecycle by defining and executing testing strategy and ensuring test coverage across levels.

### Responsibilities
- Define the test strategy for features (unit, integration, end-to-end, performance, regression)
- Create and maintain automated test suites and test harnesses
- Validate acceptance criteria before marking work as Done
- Maintain test environments and coordinate test data needs
- Triage defects, communicate quality status, and ensure timely resolution
- Coordinate manual QA where automation is not feasible

### Goals
- Ensure high confidence in releases through automated and repeatable testing
- Reduce production defects and shorten feedback cycles
- Make testing part of the development workflow (shift-left)

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups with delivery team as needed
- Test results in PRs and CI reports
- Weekly summary of test coverage and open defects to PM/PD

---

## Product Lead

### Role Summary
The Product Lead provides strategic direction across related initiatives and resolves cross-functional prioritization when needed.

### Responsibilities
- Set strategic priorities for product areas and initiatives
- Resolve cross-team prioritization conflicts and trade-offs
- Validate project one-pagers and success metrics
- Mentor Product Managers and support stakeholder negotiations
- Represent product strategy in stakeholder and executive forums

### Goals
- Align multiple projects to strategic outcomes
- Maximize impact across product investments
- Ensure consistent measurement of success metrics

### Typical Communication
- Weekly or bi-weekly check-ins with Product Managers
- Periodic stakeholder and executive updates
- Reviews at key decision gates (initiation, planning, release)

---

## Sponsor / Executive Stakeholder

### Role Summary
The Sponsor (executive stakeholder) provides approval authority and ensures the project aligns with business objectives and resourcing.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project charters and business cases
- Allocate resources (budget, personnel, and tools) as needed
- Escalate and resolve business-impacting risks and blockers
- Make final decisions on scope, timeline, and major trade-offs
- Advocate for the project within the broader organization

### Goals
- Ensure the project delivers measurable business value
- Remove organizational impediments that block progress
- Protect project priority and funding

### Typical Communication
- Executive summaries at key milestones
- Approvals for major scope or resourcing changes
- Escalation path for critical, business-impacting issues

---

## Engineering Lead

### Role Summary
The Engineering Lead provides technical leadership across the team, guiding architecture, implementation approaches, and technical debt decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Set technical direction and architecture decisions for the project
- Lead technical design reviews and mentor developers
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and dependencies
- Define coding standards and ensure best practices are followed
- Oversee release readiness from a technical perspective

### Goals
- Ensure scalable, maintainable system design
- Balance delivery speed with long-term technical health
- Reduce production incidents through good engineering practices

### Typical Communication
- Architecture and design sessions with engineers and product
- Technical risk reports in weekly syncs
- Code review and pull request guidance

---

## Security Officer / Security Lead

### Role Summary
The Security Officer ensures projects meet security and compliance requirements and leads incident response for security issues.

### Responsibilities
- Perform security reviews and threat modeling during planning
- Provide secure design guidance and approve security controls
- Ensure automated security scanning and security gating in CI
- Respond to and investigate security incidents
- Coordinate with Security and Compliance teams for audits and controls

### Goals
- Reduce security risk and ensure compliance with policies
- Integrate security practices into the delivery lifecycle
- Ensure timely response and remediation for vulnerabilities

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews during design and PR reviews
- Regular updates to PM/Eng Leads on outstanding security findings
- Incident communications to on-call/security teams when needed
